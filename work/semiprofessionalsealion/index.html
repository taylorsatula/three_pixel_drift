Semi-Professional Sea Lion// Web, CMS, Analytics · 2014

CASE STUDY AVAILABLE ON FORRST Semi-Professional Sea Lion is a personal project I've been working on for a while. I use it to curate the loveliest photos I find online. Its a collage of my favorite posts from Tumblr.

What makes websites and blogs like Wordpress or Tumblr difficult to code for is the fact that your markup has to be able to handle anything that the generator throws at it. You can't code a specific rule for an image that is the wrong size or a paragraph that is two sentences long. You need to make your one-size-fits-all approach work for everything, but at the same time make sure it doesn't look like your content is manufactured.

I enjoyed this unique challenge as it gave me an opportunity to get out of my comfort zone and explore some cool things. I also took the time to write up a case study of the project on Forrst to help the community and get feedback to continue improving my work.
