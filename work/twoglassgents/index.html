Two Glass Gents// Web, Email, Search Engine Optimization · 2012

Two Glass Gents is the premier window cleaning company of Northern Michigan. They also offer window tinting, powerwashing, and gutter cleaning.

Two Glass Gents came to me in 2012 with the request that I make them a website that would convey the professionalism they try to keep in the workplace every single day. They also needed their website to rank well in three separate locations as they have bases in Florida, Utah, and northern Michigan. This presented a unique challenge that we ended up accomplishing together thorough customized search engine optimization and boots-on-the-ground outreach.

Another facet of the development process was creating a scheduling area in partnership with Intuit wherein customers can select a time they would like to have their windows cleaned without any interaction on the phone. The ease of access really shines in comparison the competition which require you to play phone tag till the appointment is set up. This is easy and removes many unnecessary steps.

Also, TGG requested a setup of Google Apps so they could have their emails on a more professional address while retaining their ability to use Gmail and access it easily on their phones while out in the field. This setup also extended to Calenders, Contacts, and Google+
