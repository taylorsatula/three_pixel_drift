Two Glass Gents (TGG) is the premier window cleaning company of Northern Michigan. They also offer window tinting, power washing, and gutter cleaning.

TGG came to me in 2012 for a website that would convey the professionalism they bring to the workplace every single day. They also needed traffic for their other locations in Florida and Utah. Another development challenge was integrating an appointment scheduler from Intuit. This eliminated the games of phone tag our customers told us was so prevalent at other companies.

TGG also migrated to Google Apps to allow easy email access in the field. This setup also extended to Calendars, Contacts, and Google+. Now workers can finish a job, check the details of their next appointment, and head to that location without ever checking in at the office.
